SupBank is an online European banking company focused on deposit accounts,
overdraft, credit and investment products. As with all existing banks.

SupBank faces the limitations of the international SWIFT and SEPA payment
systems for international and European transfers.

SupBank has decided to create its own system for storing and transmitting
banking transaction information. This new system will have the following features:
  • Instant (nearly) wires

  • Fee-less foreign exchange

  • Decentralized

  • Tamper-proof and secure

  • Seamless transition for users

  • Full transaction history
